Install On Your Phone

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome on your phone.
  2. Go to the app URL.
  3. Choose Team A or Team B.
  4. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner.
  5. Tap Add to Home screen.
  6. Tap Add to confirm.

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Go to the app URL.
  3. Choose Team A or Team B.
  4. Tap the Share button.
  5. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  6. Tap Add to confirm.
iPhone users must use Safari. Android users should use Chrome. If you pick the wrong version by mistake, reopen the main link and choose the other one.

Quick Tips

  • Tap any day on the calendar to see shift details, mark PTO or a sick day, or add a personal note.
  • Tap the month name or year at the top to instantly jump to any month or year.
  • Tap today's date at the top of the app to jump back to the current date anytime.
  • Scroll down inside the app to see this month's pay date and time card submission deadline.
  • Your PTO, sick days, and notes are private — stored only on your device, no one else can see them.
